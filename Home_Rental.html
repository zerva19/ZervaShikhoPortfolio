Home Rental

I had the opportunity to design an innovative and user-friendly mobile interface for Homerental, a company that offers home rental services.

My focus was on creating visually appealing and intuitive screens that enhance the user experience.

The design features elegant aesthetics and seamless navigation, allowing users to easily browse listings, filter options, and book their ideal homes. By prioritizing usability and creativity, I aimed to make the app not only functional but also enjoyable to use.

This project showcases my ability to merge design with user needs, ensuring that the final product stands out in the competitive rental market.
